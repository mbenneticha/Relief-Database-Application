product - Lists products that can be purchased for donation
location - Lists locations where products may be donated (e.g., countries)
customer - Lists customers who buy products for donation to others in need
cart_status - Enumerates the status of a "cart", or collection of products; e.g., "Waiting to check out", "checked out"
shipment_status - Enumerates the status of a shipment of products; e.g., "En route", "Delivered"
product_location - Relates products to the locations where they may be donated
cart - A collection of products; the products stored in a cart are given in the "cart_contents" table
cart_contents - Relates carts to products that they contain
shipment - Lists shipments of carts to locations, along with shipment status
